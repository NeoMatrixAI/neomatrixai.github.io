/how-it-works

From your Python file to a live book.

Write a strategy once. The platform validates it, runs it on schedule, executes the orders, defends the downside, and records everything — around the clock.

Build · same code, three modes

1. Backtest

Run on Zipline or our local backtest engine. Local results are cross‑validated against real live trades.

2. Pre‑flight Validate

Upload your strategy. We run a 7‑step check — syntax, security, real‑data execution — before a single order ships.

3. Go Live

Deploy in one click. Hot‑swap your strategy or config mid‑trade. Every fill is snapshot‑logged for audit.

Protect · built to be trusted with real capital

Zero‑slippage execution

Market data is pre‑fetched ~2 minutes before every run, so your orders fire the instant the clock ticks — not after a slow data pull.

Minute‑level control

Rebalance on your own schedule, and let CrashGuard react between cycles — down to a single minute, with your own logic.

Two‑layer downside protection

Set a max loss and we auto‑liquidate and close the session — backed by a second, independent enforcement layer.

Every session, on the record

The exact strategy code, config and fills are snapshotted — replay or audit any past trade, anytime.

Self‑healing by design

If a node or job dies mid‑session, the platform detects it and recovers your positions automatically — no babysitting.

Hot‑swap, no downtime

Update your strategy or config while you're live — it takes effect on the next cycle, with no restart.

All of it runs on Kubernetes — self‑healing, snapshotted for audit, and hot‑swappable mid‑trade. You bring the strategy; we keep it running.

Plus Discord alerts & team management, cross/isolated margin, and a demo mode for risk-free dry runs before you commit capital.
